Botpuplation Feedback

What's this?

This is the feedback website for the Final Year Project game project "Botpulation". Learn more about the game here


How to play the game?

Download the game here and launch "Botpulation.exe"


How to submit feedback?

  1. While in game, press "F8"
  2. Fill in your feedback and hit submit
  3. View your feedbacks on the home page
